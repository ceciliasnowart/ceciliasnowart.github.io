Cicely "Ceci" Sinclair

About the artist

Ceci is a Scottish self-taught artist, living in Barcelona. Her artistic roots emerged in her upbringing in the countryside, inspired by the human condition, nature, the stubborn but humorous Scottish mentality, and the expressive nature of art when dealing with emotion. Using art to pass through periods of injury and to process tumultuous events, her abstract and surrealist paintings draw from diverse influences, including street art, photography, and the dynamic music of Africa and South/Central America. Her creative process is spontaneous or fluid, with works often evolving years later. Using mainly acrylics, spray paint, and markers, her aim is to connect with our human experience via colour and gaze, painting feelings we all share.

Red Era

Red Era

Ceci’s first solo show “Red Era” is the culmination of work created over the last 4 years, on moving to Barcelona. The work is an exploration of colour, form, and feeling, representing a period filled with warmth and joy, as well as intense, turbulent emotions. Ceci’s fiery use of colour and emphasis on facial expressions aims to draw attention to the hidden emotions and often unspoken truths that we obscure in everyday life; the masks we wear. In her words “I paint to convey our humanity, to process the madness, paint what I love, and to add colour and diversion to life. Art can reflect our own emotions, in a less confronting manner. If art is therapy, then welcome to my practice”
